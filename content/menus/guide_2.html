Changing the Buttons

Every button on the main menu is a 3D model. To change the buttons, it is as simple as editing the 3D model in the right spot.
Every button has 6 animations, for:
Entering
Exiting while selected
Entering (again)
Exiting while not being selected
Initial animation played when hovering over
Loop animation while hovering over
Certain buttons in Brawl have effects that loop only once. For example, the names button under the versus menu plays an animation of a pencil drawing a line, taking place in the MenMainIconXXXX_TopN__4 animation. Generally aim to keep animations the same frame count as the originals, as different lengths have not been experimented with.
