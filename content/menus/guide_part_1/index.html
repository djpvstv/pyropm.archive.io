Menu Editing Guide - Changing the Buttons

Every button on the main menu is a 3D model. To change the buttons, it is as simple as editing the 3D model in the right spot.
Every button has 6 animations, for:

Entering while not selected (MenMainIcon0001_TopN__0)
Exiting while not selected (MenMainIcon0001_TopN__1)
Entering while selected (MenMainIcon0001_TopN__2)
Exiting while selected (MenMainIcon0001_TopN__3)
Initial animation played when hovering over (MenMainIcon0001_TopN__4)
Loop animation while hovering over (MenMainIcon0001_TopN__5)

Certain buttons in Brawl have effects that loop only once. For example, the names button under the versus menu plays an animation of a pencil drawing a line, taking place in the MenMainIconXXXX_TopN__4 animation. Generally aim to keep animations the same frame count as the originals, but they can vary in length.

All animations can have clr0 and srt0 animations paired with them, named to match the animation they are played with.

This guide will cover the basics of editing the main menu and changing layouts. The guide is split up into sections, and you can jump to different sections depending on what you are trying to do.
